Our Story of Flavor

Established in 2018, Dorillo’s Inasal started with a simple passion: serving authentic, flame-grilled Filipino favorites made with heart. What began as a small neighborhood grill has grown into a beloved spot for families and friends to gather.

Our Mission

To serve freshly grilled, high-quality meals that bring people together through unforgettable taste and warm hospitality.

Our Vision

To become the go-to destination for the best inasal experience in our community, where every visit feels like home.
